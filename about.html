ABOUT US


On Time Courier


Our company would like to introduce ourselves to our UK clients as an exclusive and one of the fastest Overseas Courier Services business. We are increasing at a rapid pace and attempting to capture most of the market shares in our respective categories of services we serve. Our Web Order system requires as little as 5 clicks to complete an order. Immediate support will be available to you via online chat and phone calls. Our company has more than 250 permanent clients, and some of our clients also do online business on websites like Amazon, eBay, etc. We also provide special business accounts which give special advantages and different rates.

In reaction to enhanced demand for a convenient, flexible and inexpensive solution for customer service, OnTimeCourier was introduced a few years ago. OnTimeCourier is now one of the leading internet shipping alternatives for anyone who wants to send a parcel. OnTimeCourier’s two-day service provides an efficient and inexpensive solution to other UK carriers through its nationwide network of OnTimeCourier ParcelShops and a three-day delivery service gathered from the doorstep.

Our driver phone app enables drivers to dictate voice messages to our departments of service and operations. All it takes is just one call, and you can be sure that it will handle your shipment efficiently. We collect products from the point of origin, arrange transport and load safe records and licenses handled with government and non-governmental entities as required, track cargo movement, notify the consignor of shipment arrival at the designated port, and deliver shipments to the doorstep or warehouse of the consignor.

After the OnTimeCourier service name has been created, we enjoy significant assistance from industry partners and friends. Our demonstrated sales records allow us to negotiate competitive prices from significant shipping lines and carriers, which our clients enjoy in turn.

We ensure the safety of the goods entrusted to our care by having our staff go with the delivery vehicles to supervise personally the loading and unloading of the cargo. We have experts working with United Kingdom customs compliance to manage your imports and export shipments.

OnTimeCourier is specialized in the shipping industry for delivering at rapid times. We are not a standard courier business overnight, but a leading supplier service. We believe that we are an extension of your business.

We have a strong business association with our agents throughout the country. Our actions are always guided by the principle that “Trust is something that can not be purchased but earned”, which is why we make sure we are dealing with trusted, skilled and reliable partners.

OnTimeCourier was launched in 2009 in response to increased demand for a convenient, flexible and affordable consumer delivery solution. OnTimeCourier is now one of the leading online delivery solutions for anyone wanting to send a parcel.
